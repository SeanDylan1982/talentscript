Welcome to TalentScript. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your personal information, especially in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("POPIA") for South African citizens and anyone handling data in South Africa.

By using TalentScript, you confirm that you have read and agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Personal Information We Collect
TalentScript does not store any user data on our own servers. When you save or submit your resume data, it is stored securely on a MongoDB server located in Cape Town, managed by MongoDB, a reputable service provider.

We may collect:
- Your email address (if you provide it for feedback or support)
- Resume data you enter and choose to save
- Feedback or support messages you submit

2. Why We Collect Your Data
With your consent, we use your information to:
- Provide and improve the TalentScript resume builder service
- Respond to inquiries and feedback
- Ensure the security and reliability of the app

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Security
While no online service can guarantee 100% security, your data is stored on a secure server managed by MongoDB in Cape Town. Only authorized personnel have access, and only as needed to maintain the service.

4. Data Retention
Your information is stored only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

5. Log Files & Analytics
We may collect non-identifiable technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and usage statistics) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is tied to this data unless you submit it voluntarily.

6. Cookies
TalentScript may use cookies for core functionality and to enhance your experience. You can manage or delete cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links
TalentScript may link to external platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

8. Legal Disclosures
We comply with POPIA and other applicable laws. For any questions or clarifications, please submit a feedback ticket via the app.
